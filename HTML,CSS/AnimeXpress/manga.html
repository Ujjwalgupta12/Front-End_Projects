About AnimeXpress

AnimeXpress was built with a passion for anime and a vision to bring fans together in one place to explore, search, and enjoy their favorite titles. Whether you're into action-packed adventures, emotional dramas, or mysterious thrillers, there's something here for every otaku!

This project is developed and maintained by UJJWAL GUPTA, an anime enthusiast and aspiring web developer. AnimeXpress is a labor of love, designed to enhance your anime journey with a sleek interface and easy navigation.

Enjoy the ride and stay otaku!
